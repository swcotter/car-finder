You are a Porsche Guy You might think you are a Ferrari guy, or a Lambogini guy but at the end of the day you are a Porsche guy.
Ever since you saw that first episode of Top Gear on BBC America of Jeremy Clarkson sliding a supercar, while he yells "POWER"
You have had dreams of wanting to do the same thing. You could buy a Mazda Miata but then you would be the slow car on the track.
With a Porsche you would not only be competeive with everyone else at the local track day, but you would also have years of Porsche
racing heritage backing you up. The only downside of being a Porsche guy is the cost of entry both on the new and second hand market
and the cost of repairing your Porsche when you eventually break it because you tried to show off to your buddies one night.
