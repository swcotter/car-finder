You are a Camaro man
What can be said about this except you are a true patriot. A Chevrolet Camaro is as American as Apple Pie and fireworks on the
fourth of July. You are someone who loves performance but doesn't care about going around corners safely. This is because in America
there is only one type of car racing, its NASCAR and they only focus on straight line speed and the occasional left turn. You are someone
who lives in the country now or will be living in the country soon. An ideal date night for you and your partner would be to visit one of
three privately owned restraunts in town, or the local Olive Garden next to the Interstate. You have a six disc CD changer in your Camaro
and at least one of the CDs in it is a Bob Seger CD, most likely the Night Moves album. You are also the type of person that would spend
every Sunday, rain or shine, washing and vacuuming his Camaro.
